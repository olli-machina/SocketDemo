Instructions:

Open 2 instances of program, type 'n' in one window and 'y' in the other.
Press 'space' to spawn a boulder in a random location. 
Press 'right arrow key' to delete a boulder.
Press 'left arrow key' to spawn bees that fly across the screen while buzzing.
Press 'b' to spawn a bubble in the user's color that floats down the screen.
Press 'esc' to exit.
